How to Navigate Our Website

  • Home Page: See a welcome message and featured design image.
  • Gallery Page: View photos of latest fashion designs and their details.
  • Booking Page: Fill out the form to book a styling appointment with your name, service type, preferred time, and extras.
  • Help Page: Read instructions on using each page easily.
  • About Page: Learn about the designer and project purpose.

Form Instructions

  • Fill in your full name and select a service type.
  • Choose one preferred time slot.
  • Tick any extras if you want makeup or accessories included.
  • Add comments for special requests before submitting.

If you need more help, please contact us through the Booking page form.
